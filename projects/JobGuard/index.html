Core Features

More than analysis — a complete job search companion

From job post to offer letter, JobGuard helps you identify risks and track progress every step of the way.

JD Risk Analysis

Paste or OCR-scan a job description. The engine matches 75 rules including proximity, contradiction, frequency, and structural anomaly detection — generating a trust score with evidence chains.

Application Kanban

Track applications through stages: Applied, Contacted, In Interview, Offered. Filter by status, date, and keyword. Swipe to edit or delete with confirmation dialogs.

Interview Calendar

Built-in month calendar with interview tracking. Link interviews to applications or create standalone records. Round, format, location, and result — all captured.

Advanced Reports PRO

Funnel charts, conversion trend analysis, channel comparisons, salary distributions — visualize your job search data to find blind spots and optimize your strategy.

Home Screen Widget

Today's interview count and next upcoming interview — at a glance, without opening the app. Small and medium widget sizes supported.

Risk Reports & Smart Questions

Every matched rule comes with evidence snippets and positions in the original JD. Plus auto-generated questions you should ask HR to verify the posting.

Risk Levels

Three tiers, instant clarity

Trust scores from 0 to 100, with four severity tiers for each matched rule.

Low Risk · 71-100

No obvious red flags detected. The JD appears relatively transparent — proceed with normal caution.

Medium · 41-70

Suspicious signals found. Check the suggested questions and verify key details with the employer before proceeding.

High Risk · 0-40

Critical or multiple fraud indicators detected. Strongly recommend thorough verification before taking any action.
