# About JobGuard

JobGuard is an intelligent tool designed for job seekers to identify risks, manage progress, and make smarter career decisions.

## Core Features

**JD Risk Analysis**

Paste or capture job descriptions via OCR. JobGuard's built-in rule engine analyzes text for risk signals, providing a trust score and detailed report including suspicious clauses, missing information, and suggested questions to ask HR.

**Application Tracking**

Manage all your job applications in a kanban timeline view. Track each position through every stage: Applied → Contacted → In Interview → Offered.

**Interview Calendar**

Record interview schedules linked to your applications. View daily and monthly interview overviews at a glance.

**Data Reports**

Pro users gain access to advanced charts including the application funnel, interview conversion trends, channel analysis, and salary distribution.

**Home Screen Widget**

Quickly check today's interview count and upcoming interview details right from your home screen.

**Multi-Language Support**

Switch between Chinese, English, and Japanese with a single tap.

**100% Local Processing**

All analysis runs entirely on-device. No personal data is ever collected or uploaded.
