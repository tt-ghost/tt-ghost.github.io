Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 9, 2026

JobGuard ("we", "our", or "the App") takes your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

This App is designed with local processing as a core principle. We do NOT collect, upload, or store any of the following:

  • Your job search data (applications, interviews, JD analysis results)
  • Job description text you paste or capture
  • Your personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Your device usage data or behavioral analytics
  • Your location information

All job search data is stored solely on your device. We have no access to this data.

2. Technologies We Use

Core Data — Used to store your application records, interview schedules, and analysis results locally on your device. All data exists only on your device.

Vision Framework (OCR) — When you use the OCR photo recognition feature, all image processing is performed locally on your device. No images are uploaded to any server.

StoreKit — Used to process Pro purchases and restore purchases. Payment transactions are handled entirely by Apple; we do not receive your payment information.

WidgetKit — Home screen widgets read local data through the App Group shared container. Data never leaves your device.

UserDefaults — Used to store your app preferences (language, theme, etc.) locally on your device.

3. Third-Party Services

This App does NOT use any third-party analytics frameworks, advertising SDKs, or data tracking services.

4. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, data security depends on your device's security measures. We recommend setting a passcode or biometric lock on your device and keeping your iOS system updated to the latest version.

5. Children's Privacy

This App is rated 4+ years of age. The App does not collect personal data from anyone, and no user's personal data is collected under any circumstance.

6. Your Rights

You may delete this App from your device at any time to remove all local data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated policy will be published within the App.
